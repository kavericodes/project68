How many students go to welch?

Arlene Welch Elementary School is a highly rated, public school located in Naperville, IL. It has 624 students in grades K-5 with a student-teacher ratio of 15 to 1.

  • Hybrid Learning

    Indian Prairie 204 is increasing the amount of in-person learning for our students. Beginning on March 9, students currently enrolled in the hybrid model attending in person on-campus will attend four half days per week. Students will come to school Tuesday through Friday from 9-11:30am. Remote students will continue receiving live instruction remotely from 12:30-3:30pm. Art, Music and PE classes will take place in the afternoon, which is why the PM block is 30 minutes longer. Both remote and in-person students will participate in specials classes in the afternoon. There are a few exceptions due to scheduling constraints. Based on space availability, parents may have the option to switch from remote to in-person instruction. We will add students where space (physical space and class size limitations) is available using a lottery system because we may not be able to accommodate all students due to physical distancing space limitations in our classrooms.

  • Free Lunches

    Organic Life and District 204 continue serving free meals to all children to help ensure children have access to nutritious food as the COVID-19 pandemic continues. These meals include a lunch and a breakfast for the following day and are available for ALL students and families. Since many of our students are attending school in person, we would like to offer the option of delivery of meals to students prior to dismissal each day. If you are interested in having your hybrid child(ren) sent home with meals, please complete the form below by 4:00 PM on Thursday, March 4th, Please note that only one form is necessary per family and that these deliveries will be in place for the remainder of the year. Parents can always reach out to Mrs. Nowak if changes are needed! Families of students who selected Remote and/or families who would like to pick up meals themselves can do so each day between 12:00 - 1:30 PM in the front circle drive of Welch.
